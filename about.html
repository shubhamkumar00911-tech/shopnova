About ShopNova

Our Story

Founded in 2026, ShopNova started with a simple vision: to connect Indian consumers with high-quality, innovative products from around the world at affordable prices.

What began as a small passion project has grown into a trusted e-commerce platform serving over 10,000+ happy customers across the country. We believe that shopping should be seamless, secure, and enjoyable, which is why we obsess over every detail of the customer experience.
